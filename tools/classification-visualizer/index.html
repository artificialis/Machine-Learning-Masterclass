Classification Visualizer

How to use

  • Add data points: Click on the canvas to add blue points. Hold the Shift key and click to add red points.
  • Load presets: Choose a preset from the dropdown and click "Load".
  • Train a model: Select an algorithm from the dropdown and click "Train". The decision boundary will be visualized on the canvas.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear all data and the trained model.
